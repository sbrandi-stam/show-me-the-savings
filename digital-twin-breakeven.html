Model. bill = area × EUI × price. gross savings = bill × savings%. net = gross − OpEx. CapEx = fixed + per·area. OpEx = fixed + per·area. payback = CapEx / net. NPV = −CapEx + net × (1 − (1+r)^−T) / r. Savings, CapEx and OpEx are kept as free inputs on purpose: published twin costs run from tens of thousands to several million euros depending on scope, so a single headline number is meaningless and the honest move is to map the break-even surface.

Default anchors. Office EUI 100 to 200 kWh/m²·yr (final energy), typical European stock.
