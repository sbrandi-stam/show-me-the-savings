Model. Value created per area = EUI × price × energy% + maintenance cost × maintenance% [€/m²·yr]. net = area × value created − OpEx. CapEx = fixed + per·area. OpEx = fixed + per·area. payback = CapEx / net. NPV = −CapEx + net × (1 − (1+r)^−T) / r. The y-axis sums the two savings streams in euros, the only honest way to add a percentage of the energy bill to a percentage of the maintenance budget. The break-even line is the value the twin must create at each building size; it bends because fixed costs amortise over area, flattening to a floor set by the per-area costs. Savings and costs are free inputs on purpose: published twin costs span tens of thousands to several million euros, so the honest move is to map the surface rather than quote a number.

Default anchors. Office EUI 100 to 200 kWh/m²·yr (final energy). Non-household electricity in Italy ~0.20 to 0.25 €/kWh incl. taxes (Eurostat H2 2025; EU avg 0.184). Office maintenance budget ~20 to 25 €/m²·yr, repairs and preventive only, excluding utilities and cleaning (IFMA, BOMA, BSRIA). Realized energy savings from operational optimization roughly 10 to 30%; twin-driven maintenance cost reductions roughly 10 to 18%. Higher figures in vendor and simulation literature are best-case.

Sources. Eurostat electricity prices · BSRIA O&M maintenance benchmarks · NIST AMS 100-61, Economics of Digital Twins
